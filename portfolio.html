bunny in forest project

I made this scene because I wanted to take a school assignment beyond the scope of the assignment to make something visually more interesting than just a blank untextured model of an animal. This includes rigging, texturing, fur grooming, shading, and building a scene around it. The foliage assets and tree stump were taken from the Quixel Megascans Library to give the scene more detail. Overall I learned a lot about hair systems and hair grooming through this project.

Realistic Chess Set

This chess set was made as part of a school project. The task was to model a set of chess pieces based on a series of side-profile reference drawings. Once I was satisfied with the models, I applied textures and materials to the surfaces, positioned the chess pieces and placed area lights to give the scene a soft lightsource.

Realistic wii remote

A self-imposed modeling challenge to create a realistic Nintendo Wii Remote with the aid of various references. The texture for the buttons was created in Illustrator. This model was created in 2 days.

My Logo

This is my logo, used to represent myself as a graphic designer. I chose a simplistic deer because I personally like the animal. The rounded design is deliberate in order to appear more approachable and friendly. I based the logo off of an earlier drawing and vectorized it in Illustrator.

Sleek Stream Overlay

This is a so called "streaming overlay", which is essentially an overlay for live-streamers on platforms like twitch to give their streams their personal touch while also giving the viewers something pleasing to look at. In our briefing with my client I determined the goals as the following:
Create a monochrome minimalist yet also modern design to give off a professional look, with animated elements. In total 5 seperate screens, an animated looping background, and scene transitions were created for the client. The triangle pattern was inspired by a building in my city.
