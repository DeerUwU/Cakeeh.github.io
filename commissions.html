I remain the right to decline commissions for any reason.

Pricing:

I will typically charge 10€ for a full piece, but I might adjust pricing depending on the scope of the project. Currently I only accept payment via Paypal.

What to expect:

The process starts by discussing your ideas, wishes and requests for the commission. Once we have agreed on everything, I will begin making a sketch and show you the progress. I will make pose and idea adjustments in this stage if requested. Once you're satisfied, I will ask for payment and finish the piece afterwards. You will receive the full resolution image and project files.
